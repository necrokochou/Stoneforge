Controls:
Press A key to move left.
Press D key to move right.
Press Space key to jump. Twice for double jump.
Click left mouse button to attack or destroy objects.
Press E key to interact with certain objects.
Ctrl + S to save game.


Goal:
Mine rock piles to get Gems.
Take down enemies (optional).
Interact with altar to win the game (requires you to have at least three gems).


Debug:
F1 key to enable debug mode.
Press R key to reset the player position.
Press T key to teleport player. Hold to "drag" player.
Press Y key to force respawn.
Press U key to play a very special get-damaged voice line.
